Solmate may collect information submitted through forms, including name, phone number, email address, service category, location details, and project messages.

Information may be used to respond to requests, organize solar provider-matching context, improve site functionality, and communicate about requested services.

Providers are independent companies. If homeowner information is shared with or submitted to a provider, that provider may have its own privacy practices.
